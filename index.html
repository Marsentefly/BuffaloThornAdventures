About Us

At Buffalo Thorn Adventures, we are passionate about providing our guests with a unique and unforgettable outdoor experience. Our customised tents are designed to offer comfort and luxury in the midst of the beautiful mountains. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a family vacation, we have something for everyone.

Our team of experienced guides will take you on thrilling adventures, from 4x4 routes to motorbike trails and fishing trips. And when you're ready to relax, you can enjoy a delicious meal around the fire pit or take a leisurely stroll through the mountains.
